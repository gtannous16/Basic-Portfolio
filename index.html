    • About Me

    Gabriella Tannous was raised by her loving parents in Sugar Land, Texas. She studied Business Marketing at The University of St. Thomas in Houston, Texas. During her time in college she interned for Saks Fifth Avenue in their Marketing office.

    On June 16, 2013, Gabriella married her husband and supported him as he answered his calling to the Orthodox Christian Priesthood. Since then, they have lived in New York, and Fort Lauderdale, before finally settling down in Atlanta, Georgia where they currently live with their adorable dog Kallie!

    Gabriella is the Youth Director of the Diocese of Miami & the Southeast in the Antiochian Orthodox Christian Archdiocese, she enjoys working with, and guiding the teens and believes that the Youth is the future!

    Gabriella loves to be on her computer spending hours geeking out and writing code. She also loves reading, watching movies, building LEGO’s and has a serious obsession with Harry Potter! Being creative and artistic is an enormous passion of hers and she embraces any chance she’s blessed with to make an idea into a reality!
